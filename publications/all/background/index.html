Critical Notice

Kit Fine

This review of Lewis’s <i>Counterfactuals</i> makes an indirect appeal to the intuitive idea behind the exact truthmaker clauses in the context of the problem of disjunctive simplification in counterfactuals (p. 454). But he doesn’t go into much detail nor does he make reference to the formal clauses. The paper doesn’t reference (Fraassen 1969).
