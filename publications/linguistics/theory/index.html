Routley Star and Hyperintensionality

Sergei Odintsov and Heinrich Wansing

Compares HYPE to Heyting-Ockam logic. Contains a characterization of HYPE as identical to a modal system introduced by Moisil. From the perspective of truthmaker semantics, these results can be understood as results about (a certain kind of) inexact truthmaker semantics.

Impossible Worlds

Francesco Berto and Mark Jago

Mainly on impossible worlds but contains remarks on truthmaker semantics in a couple of places: (i) the discussion on p. 30 outlines an argument for the unsuitability of truthmaker semantics for intentional purposes; (ii) the discussion on p. 77 presents an argument against the existence of non-obtaining states of affairs based on truthmaker-considerations (with reference to earlier work by Jago); (iii) §6.5 (p. 139–140) contains a discussion of a truthmaker of the ternary relation in the semantics for relevance logic, based on/referencing (Jago 2013).
